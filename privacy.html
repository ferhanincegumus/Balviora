Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This is a starting template for an early-stage brand, not a final legal document. Before collecting real customer data, have it reviewed by a lawyer familiar with the privacy laws that apply to you (for example GDPR in the EU or KVKK in Türkiye).

1. Who we are

Balviora ("we," "us," "our") is a natural wellness brand beginning its journey with the Black Sea Balance collection. This policy explains what information we collect through this website and how we handle it.

2. What we collect

Right now, this website collects two kinds of information:

  • Email address — if you submit the "Join the Journey" form, we collect the email address you provide so we can send you updates about Balviora.
  • Usage data — anonymized information about how visitors use the site (pages viewed, general location, device type), collected through analytics tools, to help us understand and improve the site.

We do not currently sell products or process payments on this site, so we do not collect payment information.

3. How we use your information

  • To send you updates about Balviora, if you've asked to join our mailing list.
  • To understand how people use the site so we can improve it.
  • To respond if you contact us directly.

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

4. Third-party services

We may use third-party services to operate this site and communicate with you — for example, an email marketing platform to manage our mailing list, or an analytics provider to understand site traffic. These providers process data on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy policies.

5. Cookies

This site may use cookies or similar technologies from analytics providers to understand aggregate visitor behavior. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

6. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you, and to withdraw consent to marketing emails at any time by using the unsubscribe link in any email we send, or by contacting us directly.

7. Data retention

We keep your email address for as long as you remain subscribed to our mailing list, or until you ask us to delete it.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to hello@balviora.com.
